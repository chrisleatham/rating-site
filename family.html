Family Friendly

Requiem for a Dream

Rated G | 102 min | Family-Friendly | 15 December 2000

The Story of four Coney Island friends as they live fun adventures and dabble in different businesses. Often used as an educational film, Requiem for a Dream teaches young children different lessons in business and transactions, sexual education, how to be your own boss, and to always strive to be your best. Academy acclaimed actors Jared Leto and Marlon Wayans teach you the true meaning of friendship along with Jennifer Connelly teaching you what it is like to love someone.
