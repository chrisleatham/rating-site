Comedy

SAW

PG-13 | 103 min | Comedy | 29 October 2004

A friend accidentally locks his two best friends in a room together with no way out. He must now teach them ways to solve puzzles in order to break free in this laugh-out-loud goofy comedy. Staring Paul Blart, Adam Sandler, and Rob Schneider as Jigsaw, this film will have you in stitches from beginning to end.

Family Friendly

Requiem for a Dream

Rated G | 102 min | Family-Friendly | 15 December 2000

The Story of four Coney Island friends as they live fun adventures and dabble in different businesses. Often used as an educational film, Requiem for a Dream teaches young children different lessons in business and transactions, sexual education, how to be your own boss, and to always strive to be your best. Academy acclaimed actors Jared Leto and Marlon Wayans teach you the true meaning of friendship along with Jennifer Connelly teaching you what it is like to love someone.

Action/Adventure

Toy Story

R | 81 min | Action/Adventure | 22 November 1995

Join the roughest and toughest cowboy as he fights the meanest and fiercest astronaut that has entered his territory. Directed by Quentin Tarantino comes the greatest action movie that Jason Statham has ever been in. Toy Story brings the true grit that you wished that True Grit had. Enjoy abandonment, lasers, monster baby-spiders, and the claw.

Horror

Boo to You Too!

Rated X | 21 min | Horror | 25 October 1996

The Story of four friends who go through a neverendless field of trees on halloween for some holiday fun. Little do they know, while they are out there, they are being stalked by what will turn into their violent demise. Directed by Guillermo Del Toro, this horror flick will truly teach you to be afraid of visiting the Hundred Acre Wood.

Documentary

Kung Fury

PG-13 | 31 min | Documentary | 28 May 2015

The true story along side renegade cop Kung Fury. This shockingly true story takes you to the time where Kung Fury travels back in time to kill the worst criminal of all time - kung fuhrer Hitler. Join Kung Fury as he hacks through time and meets Thor and other assorted characters that explain true events.
